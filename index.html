Turn your steps into a garden.

Gide - Stride is a calm walking game where movement becomes dewdrops, dewdrops grow flowers, and flowers fill peaceful gardens.

A softer reason to move

Progress without pressure.

Stride makes healthy movement feel like tending something beautiful. Instead of streak anxiety or noisy fitness charts, you collect dewdrops, plant seeds, unlock blooms, and build gardens at your own pace.

Private by design

Inside the app

A calm garden in your pocket.

Privacy-first

Your garden stays yours.

Gide - Stride is designed as an offline game. It does not require an account, and game progress is stored on your device. Step counts are read on-device when step tracking is enabled.
